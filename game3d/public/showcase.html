STARS 3D

A full 3D arcade game that runs at 60 fps on a phone, loads in seconds, and ships zero external assets — every texture, character and note of music is generated in code.

▶ Play the demo No install, no account — works on desktop, mobile & gamepad. Leave it idle and it demos itself.

One skill: timing

Hold to pump your swing, let go at the peak, flip mid-air, catch the next bar. Releases are graded PERFECT / GOOD / OK — easy to learn, endlessly satisfying to master. Failure is never punished: a safety net, soft respawns and generous windows keep young players flying.

Built like a product

Not a tech demo — a complete arcade loop with retention, accessibility and shareability baked in.

🎬

CINEMATIC INTRO

Fly-through opening with spotlights firing in sequence and an animated logo. Skippable on first input.

🕹

ATTRACT MODE

Left idle, the game plays itself like an arcade cabinet — perfect for a stand or a shop window.

📅

DAILY CHALLENGE

A date-seeded rail, identical for every player in the world that day, with its own daily best.

🏆

ARCADE SCORING

Combos, medals per world, endless mode with rising difficulty, top-10 board with 3-initial entry.

🎵

GENERATIVE AUDIO

One synthesized soundtrack per world plus 14 sound effects — pure WebAudio, zero audio files.

♿

ACCESSIBLE

Respects reduced-motion, offers a reduce-flashes toggle, keyboard focus and HUD contrast passes.

📸

PHOTO FINISH

Your best catch is captured automatically and shareable via the native share sheet.

🎆

GRAND FINALE

Fireworks and a full ovation when you complete the tour or take the #1 spot.
